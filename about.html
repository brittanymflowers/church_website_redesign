About Us

Who we are and what we believe

Woodmen Road Church is a conservative and fundamental church located at the southeastern corner of Black Forest and Woodmen Road. Our church is easily accessible from anywhere in the greater Colorado Springs, Black Forest, or Falcon areas. Our church facilities have recently been beautifully renovated and landscaped.

We are a self-governing body of believers. We believe that the head of the church is the Lord Jesus Christ. The Bible is our sole authority for faith and practice. It is our desire to be a truly "New Testament" church, taking the gospel "around the corner, and around the world." Without apology, we are a traditional, conservative, and fundamental church. We by design sing the "old time hymns of the faith" and stand firm on the "old time preaching" of the Word of God!

At Woodmen Road, we have one message - the message of the Lord Jesus Christ, and one ministry - the ministry He has given us to go into all the world and preach the gospel to every creature. We believe this can be obtained through fervent soul-winning, preaching, bus ministry, Sunday school, youth ministry, men and women's ministry, missions at home and around the world, and any other Christ-honoring method.
